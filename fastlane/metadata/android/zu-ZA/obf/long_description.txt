*** Nika isandla ekuziphatheni ngokuziqhenya ***

- I-Open Food Facts iyinhlangano yemininingwane yokudla eyenziwe yilowo nalowo, wonke umuntu.
- Ungayisebenzisa ukwenza ukhetho olungcono kakhulu, futhi njengoba livulekile idatha, noma ubani angayisebenzisa kabusha nganoma iyiphi injongo.
- I-Open Beauty Facts yinhlelo engeyona inzuzo eyenziwa yizinkulungwane zamavolontiya avela emhlabeni wonke. We have lots of exciting projects you can contribute to in many different ways. 
- Ungaqala ukufaka isandla namhlanje ngokungeza umkhiqizo kusuka ekhabetheni lakho lezithombeni. 

*** Get the Facts ***

- Izithasiselo ze-Cosmetics, i-Fil. amakhodi, izinombolo ze-CI, i-INCI (I-International Nomenclature yezithako ezenziwe ngama-cosmetic), uhlu lwezithako, izilonda, amakhodi wokupakisha... 
- I-Open Beauty Facts ikusiza ukuba wenze umqondo wokuphrinta okuhle kumalebula womkhiqizo.
- Akudingeki ube yikhemistri futhi ufunde latin uphinde usebenzise i-decypher uhlu lwezithako :-)
- Ungakwazi ukuskena: emva kokuhamba, umzimba we-lotions, i-creams, i-deodorant, eyeliner, isisekelo, iphunga, i-lipstick, i-makeup, i-mask, i-perfume, i-shampoo, insipho, i-gel yokugeza, i-nail polish nokuningiâ€¦

*** Isebenza kahle nganoma yisiphi isimo ***
- Ungangeza imikhiqizo emisha, noma ngabe awunawo uxhumano lwe-inthanethi okwamanje.
- Uhlelo lokusebenza luthwala kuphela okudingekayo okunciphisa ukunciphisa idatha kanye nokusetshenziswa kwebhethri
- Awudingi ikhamera ukuze ubuke umkhiqizo. Ungaphinda uthayiphe ibhakhodi uma ungenayo ikhamera noma unenkinga nayo.
